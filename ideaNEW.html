ABOUT US

Teaching Environment

Naadashankara Music Academy is Hassan's only fully equiped music school for anyone.Here at NS, we believe in making a student comletely ready as a real world musician. We adapt best and easy techniques ensuring the teaching process to be fun. Unlike any other music schools, NS is for anyone who wants to explore the beauty of music irrespective of age!

Performance

We value and encourage every little idea of a student which empowers the overall performance. Stage shows, concerts, grade examinations are always held which evaluates the performance. A keen and individual observation helping in reinforcment of a unique and creative talent within a learner.

Certified

Naadashankara Music Academy is certified as an affiliate franchise to the CHENNAI MUSIC ACADEMY.
And recognised by the National Centre for the Performing Arts, Mumbai
